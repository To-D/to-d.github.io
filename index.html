Junwei Liu

Junwei Liu is currently a PhD student in the School of Computer Science, Fudan University. As a member of CodeWisdom Group, he had the invaluable opportunity to work under the guidance and mentorship of Prof. Xin Peng and Prof. Yiling Lou. His research interests mainly focus on Software Engineering and its synergy with Artificial Intelligence, especially large language models(LLMs). Previously, he obtained his B.S. degree in Software Engineering from Fudan in 2022.
